Product: AquaPure Water Filter

FAQs:
Q: What contaminants does it remove?
A: It removes lead, chlorine, microplastics, and some bacteria.

Q: How often should I replace the filter?
A: Every 3 months or 300 liters, whichever comes first.

Q: Is it compatible with all faucets?
A: It fits most standard faucets but not pull-out or spray-style types.
