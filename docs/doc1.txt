Product: SmartHome Thermostat

FAQs:
Q: How do I install the SmartHome Thermostat?
A: Use the wall mount and follow the app-guided setup process.

Q: What is the battery life?
A: The thermostat lasts up to 12 months on two AA batteries.

Q: Can I control it remotely?
A: Yes, using the SmartHome app on iOS or Android.
